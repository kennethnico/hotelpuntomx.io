Parking

Afraid to leave your keys in the parking lot?

At Hotel Punto MX, we have a parking agreement with DEPRISA.

It means that you enter and leave our parking lots with your car without having to leave your keys to anyone, making the payment in one of our payment machines, all this, in clean, well-kept facilities and just a few steps from places and places of interest around the city.
